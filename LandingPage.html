The Problem?

We want to help people who can't speak a language while in a different country. Those who are in immediate need of a translator when distressed. So how do we help translate for people who don’t know a certain language and are in a different country?

View details »

Our Answer!

We create an app that has translators ready at anytime for anyone regardless of circumstances. Not only will we advertise for people who need this app, but for bilingual people who can use the app to help others in need and might need a little help of their own.

View details »

Starting at our First Step

When in need of help all people have to do is open the WorldTranslations app and start looking for the country they are in and select the needed langauge that is needed it's as easy as pie!

Second Step coming in!

You don't even have to do anything for this step! After you've selected your needed language our app then searches our databases for others who are bilingual that use the app and those who know the language that is needed and sees if they are available.

Finally our Last Step

After we find the perfect translator just for you we then connect both you and the translator in whatever means possible. Whether that is by text, call, or even facetime. We will make sure that you and you're translator get into contact so that the translator can help you with your problems.
