App process

: how this app has been designed and developed to solve the problem
When in need of help all people have to do is open the WorldTranslations app and start looking for the country they are in and select the needed langauge that is needed.
After you've selected your needed language our app then searches our databases for others who are bilingual that use the app and those who know the language that is needed and sees if they are available.
After we find the perfect translator just for you we then connect both you and the translator in whatever means possible. Whether that is by text, call, or even facetime. We will make sure that you and you're translator get into contact so that the translator can help you with your problems.
